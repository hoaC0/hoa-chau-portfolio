About me

Hi!

I'm Hoa Chau, a passionate student and developer who loves not only technology but also creating and learning new things about it. I love creating my own projects, challenge myself and learn new things. Currently, I am a student in Computer Science at Bernd-Blindow-Schulen in Friedrichshafen(Germany), so I can do something I am passionate about.

I started being interested in video games when I was just a little child, and eventually, I built my very first computer at the age of 16. My fascination extended beyond hardware to software. I began editing pictures and videos, which sparked my interest in programming and web development. What started as a hobby has now become my field of study. Recently, I have also developed a growing interest in AI.

When I'm not coding or studying, I also enjoy playing video games, working out and cooking.
